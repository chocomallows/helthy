Welcome to Helthy.

We will helth you on your way

How can we help you?

At Helthy, we care for and treat each client as if they were a family member.

As dietitians and nutritionists, we will do everything in our power to make sure you achieve your goal in the most efficient and sustainable way possible.

Whether you need help with weight loss, sports nutrition, a plant-based diet, child nutrition, IBS, diabetes or any other health-related condition, our dietitians and nutritionists can develop a personalised action plan to make you reach your objective.

Wa are based in Antwerpen and also offer online consultations.

What we offer

Here you can find an overview of what we can do for you and how we can work together for a healthier future!

Intake - 1hr

During this intake interview, we will determine your needs based on your specific situation. Whether it's weight loss, eating disorders or difficulties you are facing due to a medical condition, we will listen to your story and set up a plan tailored to your needs.

Cost: €50

Follow-up 30min

Every appointment subsequent to the intake interview will be 30 minutes. During these follow-ups, we determine whether or not we are still on track, if changes need to be made and if the plan we set up towards the future needs to be adjusted.

Cost: €30

We are here for your children too!

Building up new habits and making changes in your daily life can be difficult with our dearest around. This program not only sets you up for your own health goals but also offers guidance to your children to get those healthy habits going!

Cost: Intake €75 | Follow-up €45

Extra coaching

This program is more intense with more appointments than our other modules, but will make sure you are kept on track not just for immediate results but also long-term betterment! This also comes with 7/7 support.

Cost: €100

It's not all about the food

Health and wellbeing goes beyond the food we eat.

That's why we at Helthy actively incorporate topics in our programs that go beyond just your food intake. Health can also be found in your sleep, physical activity, mental wellbeing, etc.

To further guide you through this maze of (mis)information about the many do's and don'ts of a healthy lifestyle, we also organise Workshops! Feel free to check them out here.

Regardless of whether or not you decide to set up an appointment with us to build towards a healthier future, we would love to leave you with some lovely food recommendations!
