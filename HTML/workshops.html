Join our Workshop

We have 3 types of workshops :


1 - Vegan Workshop and Talks


We make healthy and plant based eating easy and tasty for the busy worker. Using our food innovation and healthy eating knowledge, we give practical guidance and advice to get healthy, improve cooking skills and eat more veg.


2 - Diabetes Education Workshop


The Introduction to Diabetes Workshop is for anyone worried about their risk of diabetes or who already have diabetes. This two-hour workshop reviews the lifestyle changes that can prevent or potentially reverse diabetes.


3 - Sport Nutrition Workshop


Do you want to know about the best food choices on the market for your health and performance? Are you unsure of which foods to eat before, during, and after training? Are you wondering when are the best times to eat for maintaining a healthy weight? In this interactive workshop you will rate your plate to see what you’re doing well and where you could make some changes to improve your health, training, and performance.
